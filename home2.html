Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be home during the repair?
Yes, we require someone to be present to unlock the bike and sign off on the completed work, unless prior arrangements (like garage access) are made.
What if my repair requires special parts?
Our vans carry hundreds of common parts. If you need something highly specific, our mechanic will diagnose the issue, order the part, and schedule a quick follow-up visit.
Do you offer warranties on your service?
Absolutely. All labor is backed by a 30-day guarantee. If something doesn't feel right after we leave, we'll make it right at no extra cost.
How long does a typical repair take?
Most basic tune-ups take 45-60 minutes. More comprehensive services like full overhauls can take 1.5-2 hours. We'll give you an estimated time when you book.
Do you have a cancellation policy?
You can cancel or reschedule free of charge up to 24 hours before your appointment. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a $25 service fee.
