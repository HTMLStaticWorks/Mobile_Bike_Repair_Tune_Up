Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a repair take?
Most standard tune-ups take between 45 to 60 minutes. Complex repairs or full overhauls can take up to 2 hours. Our mechanics work efficiently right at your location.
Do you come to my location?
Yes! We are a 100% mobile service. We come to your home, office, or even if you're stranded on a local bike path within our service area.
What if I need parts?
Our vans are stocked with hundreds of standard parts (tubes, tires, chains, cables). If you need a special part, we can order it and schedule a quick return visit to install it.
Do I pay before or after the service?
You pay only after the service is completed and you are 100% satisfied. We accept all major credit cards and digital payments on-site.
Is there a call-out fee?
We have a transparent pricing policy. A diagnostic call-out fee of $25 applies, but this fee is completely waived if you proceed with any of our listed repair services.
