Pricing FAQs

Can I customize my service?
Yes. If your bike needs specific attention not listed in our packages, we provide an a-la-carte menu and custom quotes straight from our mechanic upon arrival.
Are parts included in these prices?
No, these prices reflect mobile mechanic labor. If any parts like tubes, cables, or chains need replacing, our mechanic will provide you with options and exact costs before proceeding.
Is there a travel fee?
Travel fees are included in the visit price for most central locations. For addresses outside our standard service radius, a small surcharge may apply, which will be shown during the booking process.
